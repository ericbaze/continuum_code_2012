Careers at Continuum

We have 4 openings for smart, talented people who are interested in being a part of a funded startup pursuing disruptive technology for large-scale data analysis in science, technology, and business intelligence.

Interested in joining our team? Send us an email.

Web Developer

We are looking for several capable web developers who understand how to construct dynamic, interactive applications for the browser. They should have a familiarity with a variety of Javascript UI and MVC frameworks, but not be defined by them. We need people with the vision to imagine what is possible in a rich, SPA-type of analytics tool and who possess the expertise to implement it.

Bonus skills & expertise: Understanding web app backends; UI/UX expertise with data analysis or visualization; CSS/HTML design chops.


Data Scientist

We are looking for a motivated scientist or engineer who loves to play with large-scale data problems. The ideal candidate would be familiar with map-reduce style problems but not be defined by them. This person would have experience in a variety of technical fields but strongly characterized by an interminable curiosity for understanding the world through data. The right candidate will be responsible for promoting our products for large-scale data management and science by developing examples and demonstrations. The ideal candidate would also be able to write and speak about their experiences and insights. Experience with Python and NumPy is a must. Experience in machine learning, engineering, and/or statistical data analysis is recommended.


Build-and-Release Expert

We are looking for a smart, talented person who wants to help the NumFOCUS projects (NumPy, SciPy, Matplotlib, IPython, SymPy, etc.) and get paid to work on them up to 50% time. The remaining time will be spent building, testing, and working closely with our development team on our internal cutting-edge products. We need someone who will both manage the release and builds of NumPy itself and also Continuum's internal products. If you haven't heard of Continuous Integration or Jenkins or Hudson or ShiningPandas, then no need to apply. You need to be smart, self-motivated, and willing to learn new things.


Senior Developer

We are looking for an experienced, talented programmer with NumPy, Python, or other equivalent programming experience. Depending on your skills, you will work on one of our core products or technologies, including our next-generation data management products which will push Python and its vectorized enhancements such as NumPy into general use in big data analytics. Must have 4+ years Python and C/C++ development experience and 2+ years experience with NumPy or the equivalent.

Bonus skills & experience: Distributed databases, parallel computation, KDB+, Gemstone, OLAP/MDX, LLVM.
